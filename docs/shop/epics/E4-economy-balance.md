# E4 — Economy and Balance Pass

## Goal

Tune oxygen/currency/hazard pacing so progression feels meaningful and bottlenecks are intentional.

## Scope

- Define early/mid/late progression targets.
- Tune oxygen spawn baseline, refill value, hazard pressure, and upgrade deltas.
- Consolidate tuning constants into a clear balance config.

## Deliverables

- `utils/balance/*` or central tuning constants
- Balance worksheet in `docs/shop/balance/`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Players feel measurable improvement after upgrades.
- Progression avoids trivialization while reducing hard stalls.
- Tuning values are centralized and documented.

## Dependencies

- E2 and E3 complete.

## Out of Scope

- Backend sync implementation.
